Best Paper Award Image Dehazing Super-Resolution

Project Overview (2022/12-2023/8)

This project, which received the **Best Paper Award at IEEE IS3C 2023**, investigates the synergy between **Image Dehazing** and **Super-Resolution (SR)** networks. The primary focus is on reducing the computational overhead of high-quality restoration.

Research & Innovation

Supported by the NSTC Research Grant (Grant NSTC 112-2813-C-033-009-E), this work explores **Joint Training** strategies where dehazing and SR tasks are optimized simultaneously. This approach allows the network to restore clarity and resolution more efficiently than two-stage pipelines.

Applications include Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS), where maintaining visibility in foggy weather is critical for safety and object recognition.
