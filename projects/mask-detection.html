Object Detection Tracking Creativity Award

Project Overview (2020/11-2022)

This project involved the development of a comprehensive **Mask Wearing Classification and People Flow Estimation** system. It received the CYCU College Student Research Creativity Award (110學年度中原大學大專學生研究計畫研究創作獎).

Technical Implementation

The system leverages advanced **Object Detection and Multi-Object Tracking** algorithms to monitor crowd density and compliance with public health mandates. By processing real-time video streams, the system provides accurate counts and identifies individuals not wearing masks correctly.

The work was published in the *Journal of Internet Technology*, showcasing its robustness in real-world public monitoring scenarios.

Key Achievements

  • **Award**: CYCU Research Creativity Award.
  • **Journal**: Journal of Internet Technology (Accepted).
  • **Keywords**: Mask Wearing Classification, People Flow Estimation, Object Tracking, Crowd Monitoring.
