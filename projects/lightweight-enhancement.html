Lightweight Deep Learning for Image Enhancement

基於深度學習的輕量級影像增強方法

Edge AI Knowledge Distillation Explainable AI

Project Overview (2023/3-2024/2)

This research investigates **Lightweight Deep Learning** methods for image enhancement, specifically tailored for **Edge AI and Mobile Devices**. The primary goal is to maintain high-quality results while significantly reducing computational requirements and memory footprint.

Technical Approach

The project utilizes **Knowledge Distillation** to transfer capabilities from large-scale teacher models to compact student networks. It also emphasizes **Explainable AI (XAI)**, providing insights into how the network makes adjustment decisions based on input image features.

The proposed method achieves real-time performance on various mobile processors, making it highly practical for smartphone photography and real-time surveillance.
